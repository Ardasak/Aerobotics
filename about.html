Our Mission

Our mission is to ignite curiosity and passion among college students by exploring the exciting world of robotics. Through the principles of first-principle thinking, we aim to tackle complex challenges and foster innovation. We are committed to leading our peers in developing groundbreaking projects and competing in the First Robotics Challenge, showcasing our technical skills and collaborative spirit.

Our Vision

Our vision is to inspire and empower the next generation of technologists by demonstrating the limitless possibilities within robotics. We strive to build a community where creativity, persistence, and teamwork drive us toward achieving uncommon results. As we advance through our journey, we aspire to make a significant impact in the field of robotics, contributing to the future of technology and encouraging others to embrace and explore their own potential.
